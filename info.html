Currency Conversion

One of the key factors that determines a nation's relative economic health is the exchange rate.

A nation's exports are more expensive in overseas markets and its imports are less expensive when its currency appreciates. Learn about the exchange rates and how to convert currencies.

Measurement Conversion

Unit of measurement can be expressed in a the same feature through unit conversion.

For example, one may express time in minutes rather than hours, and one can translate distance in miles, kilometres, feet, or any other unit of measurement. Measurements are frequently provided in one set of units, like feet, but are required in another,like chains.

A conversion factor is a mathematical equation that permits the equivalent conversion from feet to chains.

Explore the world of measurement units and convert between different units like length, weight, and volume.
